Crafting Memories, One Frame at a Time

At LunarFrame Imagery, we are more than just photographers; we are storytellers, dreamers, and creators. With a deep passion for the art of photography, we strive to capture the extraordinary in the ordinary, the magic in the mundane, and the beauty in every detail.

Founded on the belief that every moment deserves to be celebrated, LunarFrame Imagery was born from a desire to craft images that resonate with emotion and authenticity. Whether it’s the subtle glance between loved ones, the joy of a milestone, or the serenity of a quiet landscape, our goal is to encapsulate these moments in a way that speaks to the soul.

Our approach is simple: we listen, we observe, and we create. By understanding your unique vision, we tailor our craft to reflect your personality and story. With every click, we aim to produce more than just photographs—we aim to create lasting memories that you can cherish forever.

My Services

Explore the range of services designed to capture, enhance, and elevate your most memorable moments.

Photography

Our photography services are designed to capture the essence of your most cherished moments. Whether it's a wedding, family portrait, or a personal milestone, we ensure every image is a timeless keepsake that tells your unique story

Videography

Bring your memories to life with our videography services. We create cinematic experiences that capture the emotions, sounds, and movements of your special moments, delivering videos that you'll want to watch over and over again.

Photo Retouching

Perfect your images with our professional retouching and editing services. We enhance your photos to bring out their best qualities, ensuring each shot is as stunning as the moment it was taken.

Aerial Photography

Experience your world from a new perspective with our aerial photography services. Using cutting-edge drone technology, we capture breathtaking images and videos from above, providing a unique viewpoint that elevates your memories.

Lightning Setup

Achieve the perfect ambiance with our expert lighting setup services. We craft the ideal lighting conditions for your shoot, whether it's natural light or a carefully designed studio setup, ensuring every shot is beautifully lit.

Video Color Grading

Enhance the mood and tone of your videos with our professional color grading services. We meticulously adjust the colors in your footage to create a consistent and visually captivating final product that reflects your vision.
